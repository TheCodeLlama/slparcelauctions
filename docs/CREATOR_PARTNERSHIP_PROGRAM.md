# Second Life Creator Partnership Program

_Research compiled: 2026-04-09_

---

## Overview

Launched **April 23, 2025** by Linden Lab. An open call for SL creators to collaborate with LL on improving Second Life, specifically around new user experiences and community engagement.

- **Brainchild of:** Brett Linden (Marketing VP) and Steeltoe Linden (Manager of Product & UX Design)
- **Response:** Over 300 proposals submitted within the first day
- **Status:** Active and accepting proposals as of April 2026
- **Official blog post:** https://community.secondlife.com/news/featured-news/introducing-the-second-life-creator-partnership-program-r11184/

Brett Linden described it as representing "a major pivot in how the company collaborates with the SL community."

---

## Three Focus Areas

### 1. Welcome Packs

Content packages for new users to help them get started in SL.

- **Avatar Packs** (human and non-human) - Already launched with Lelutka, Addams, MeshBody, Velour
- **Clothing & Accessory Packs**
- **Home Furnishing Packs**
- Distribution: newcomer-friendly areas in-world, Marketplace, Viewer
- All content should be optimized for users not yet familiar with SL's intricacies
- Themed, seasonal, and limited edition packs planned

### 2. Community Perks

Enhanced benefits for established residents.

- Enhanced paid membership perks
- Free seasonal and special giveaways
- Mobile Daily Rewards expansion to include virtual gifts
- Future programs and promotions with virtual perks/prizes

### 3. Community Gateways

High-quality in-world experiences as newcomer-friendly destinations.

- Partners create welcoming, engaging spaces
- Featured in Community Gateways and newcomer destination lists
- Builds on the older Community Gateway program (relaunched)

---

## What Creators Get

- Brand and content exposure to a wider, loyal audience
- Potential inclusion in major onboarding and promotion campaigns
- Strategic impact - direct collaboration with LL to shape SL's future
- Direct line to LL leadership via periodic Zoom calls

## How to Apply

1. **Submit a proposal:** [Creator Partnership Program Inquiry Form](https://forms.gle/Kruy9c6FHLYvY3JN9)
2. **Sign up for Zoom calls:** [Zoom Call Signup Form](https://forms.gle/4B2gPgQ86PXLBCZw5) - periodic calls with LL leadership to learn more and ask questions directly

### What LL Is Looking For

From the official announcement:
> "Whether you're a fashion designer, avatar builder, home goods creator, experience developer, or just someone with a standout idea—we want to hear from you."

From Brett Linden's interview with New World Notes:
> "We are very open to talking to anybody and everybody, and that's evidenced in the announcement of the Creator Partnership Program, and it's genuinely an open call for collaboration. Doesn't mean that if I have 300 [applications] that I'm going to do 300 deals, of course that doesn't scale."

Community discussion suggests LL is focused on:
- Proposals that attract and retain new users
- High-quality content from established creators
- Ideas that demonstrate clear value to the platform

## Related LL Initiatives (Context)

### Creator Zoom Calls (Pre-Program)

Before the formal program launched, LL held creator Zoom meetings:
- **December 16, 2024:** Zoom with Philip Rosedale and Brad Oberwager, organized through the CSC (Content Creators of SL) Discord server
- These calls appear to be ongoing and periodic

### Avatar Welcome Packs (Already Shipped)

- Rolled out quietly before the program announcement
- Located in System Library within inventory
- Include "lite" and "basic" female heads and bodies
- Created by Lelutka, Addams, MeshBody, Velour
- This was the proof of concept that led to the full Creator Partnership Program

### Community Gateway Program (Legacy, Revived)

- Original program predates the Creator Partnership Program
- Gave organizations special status for onboarding new users
- Required: 1+ year old account, no abuse reports, good financial standing, website with SL registration
- Now folded into the Creator Partnership Program as one of the three focus areas

### Tilia / Thunes Payment Transition

- June 2025: LL's payment platform Tilia acquired by Thunes (global payments company)
- Has caused some merchant payment processing delays
- Relevant context: LL may be cautious about third-party L$ handling during this transition

## Relevance to SLPA

The Creator Partnership Program is primarily aimed at **content creators** (avatar builders, fashion designers, experience developers), not **infrastructure/marketplace builders** like SLPA. However:

### Why It's Still Relevant

1. **The door is open.** LL explicitly says "just someone with a standout idea - we want to hear from you." SLPA is a standout idea.

2. **The Zoom calls are gold.** Even if the formal program isn't the right fit, signing up for Zoom calls gets us face time with LL leadership (Brett Linden, possibly Brad Oberwager and Philip Rosedale). We can pitch SLPA directly.

3. **The form is low-cost.** Submitting a proposal takes 10 minutes. Worst case: they say "this doesn't fit the program but contact business@lindenlab.com."

4. **SLPA improves the new user experience.** New users who want land have no good way to find and purchase it. A transparent auction marketplace IS a new user experience improvement - it just happens to also serve veterans.

5. **LL wants growth.** Brad Oberwager's stated priority is growing SL's user base. A land marketplace that increases Mainland liquidity and makes land more accessible directly serves that goal.

### How to Frame SLPA for the Program

Don't pitch it as "third-party marketplace" - pitch it as:
- **"Making Mainland land accessible and discoverable for new residents"**
- A transparent, trustworthy way for newcomers to find and buy their first parcel
- Reduces the intimidation factor of SL land ownership
- Drives Mainland occupancy (which benefits ALL residents, not just land sellers)

### Recommended Approach

1. **Submit the Creator Partnership Program form** - Frame SLPA as a community experience that improves land discovery for new and existing residents
2. **Sign up for the Zoom call** - Pitch directly to LL leadership
3. **Also email business@lindenlab.com** - For the more technical/business partnership conversation (API access, tier arrangements)
4. **Do both simultaneously** - They're different channels to the same company. Let them route internally.
